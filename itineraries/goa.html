Goa Itinerary

Sun, beaches, culture & nightlife

Day 1 – North Goa

Arrive in Goa, check in to your hotel. Visit Baga, Calangute and Anjuna beaches. Enjoy water sports and sunset at Vagator Beach.

Day 2 – Culture & Heritage

Explore Old Goa churches – Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Visit Fontainhas Latin Quarter and enjoy authentic Goan cuisine.

Day 3 – South Goa

Relax at Colva and Benaulim beaches. Visit Cabo de Rama Fort for panoramic sea views.

Day 4 – Adventure & Nightlife

Dudhsagar waterfall trip or spice plantation tour. Evening Mandovi river cruise and nightlife exploration.

Day 5 – Leisure & Departure

Shopping at flea markets, beach relaxation and departure.
