Goa – 5 Day Travel Itinerary

Beaches • Nightlife • Culture

Baga Beach, Calangute, Fort Aguada, sunset at Candolim.

Parasailing, jet ski, banana ride, beach cafés.

Anjuna flea market, Tito’s Lane, beach clubs.

Day 5 – Relax & Departure

Morning beach walk, souvenir shopping.
